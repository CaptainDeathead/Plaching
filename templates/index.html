Plaching - Homepage

Plaching is a wedding photo app where people can view and upload photo's of a wedding.

FAQ's

How do I upload photo's to a wedding registered with Plaching?

The wedding hosts should have QR codes you can scan on your phone or check your emails / messages to see if you have been sent the wedding link or QR code.

Why should I use Plaching at my wedding?

Plaching allows you to get many different people's perspectives of your wedding and unique photos that aren't normally captured at a wedding.
